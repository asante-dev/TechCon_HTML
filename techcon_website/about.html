History

TechCon started in 2010 with a small group of passionate developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into an international platform for sharing breakthroughs in technology, bringing together voices from startups, academia, and major tech companies.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and inclusion in the tech industry. TechCon is committed to creating a space where people from all backgrounds can share ideas, build networks, and shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Linda Kwame

A trailblazer in AI research, Dr. Kwame is known for her work on ethical machine learning. Her keynote in 2022 challenged the industry to take responsibility for bias in algorithms.

James Soto

CEO of NextNet, James spoke on sustainable tech solutions in 2021. His talk inspired a wave of green tech startups across Africa and Southeast Asia.

Sofia Zhang

Sofia is a product designer and advocate for accessible tech. She shared powerful stories about inclusive UX design at TechCon 2023.
